# GEMINI.md

## Project

Croatian landing page for a small local IT help / computer repair service in Zagreb.

## Main Goal

Redesign the frontend so it looks like a trustworthy local service, not an AI-generated SaaS landing page.

## Visual Target

The site must feel:

- local
- honest
- calm
- practical
- readable
- human
- affordable but not cheap
- professional but not corporate

## What To Remove

Remove or heavily reduce:

- glassmorphism
- heavy gradients
- huge blur blobs
- GSAP animations in static sections
- mouse-follow effects
- floating hero cards
- excessive rounded corners
- huge shadows
- generic SaaS pricing cards
- fake “wow” effects

## What To Keep

Keep:

- Croatian language
- honest positioning
- Zagreb/local context
- clear pricing
- limitations section
- FAQ
- mobile-first layout
- simple contact flow

## Important Rules

- Work one phase at a time.
- Do not rewrite unrelated files.
- Do not add fake reviews.
- Do not add fake address.
- Do not add fake phone/email.
- Do not invent business claims.
- Prefer server components where possible.
- Use client components only for real interactivity.
- Preserve SEO basics.
- Before editing, make a short plan.
